# **Market Research Checklist – Kenyan Digital Solutions Startup**

## **1. Target Customer Analysis**

**Goal:** Identify paying customers and their needs.

- **Tasks:**
    
    - Create a list of 50–100 potential SME clients (shops, creatives, startups, service providers)
        
    - Conduct short interviews or WhatsApp surveys to capture:
        
        - Current online presence
            
        - Pain points in sales, marketing, operations
            
        - Interest in websites, social media management, automation
            
        - Budget range for digital services
            
    - Analyze results to segment clients into **Launch / Scale / Dominate** potential
        
- **Metrics to Track:**
    
    - % of businesses interested in services
        
    - Average budget per package
        
    - Common pain points (ranked by frequency)
        

---

## **2. Competitor Analysis**

**Goal:** Understand competition and identify gaps.

- **Tasks:**
    
    - List top 5 local agencies + Workke.co.ke + 5 global players (Fiverr, Upwork)
        
    - Document:
        
        - Packages offered
            
        - Pricing tiers
            
        - Delivery speed and quality
            
        - Marketing and visibility methods
            
    - Identify gaps your company can fill (trust, bundled services, local support, speed)
        
- **Metrics to Track:**
    
    - Package & price ranges
        
    - Unique selling points missing in competitors
        
    - Opportunities for differentiation
        

---

## **3. Service Viability**

**Goal:** Test what clients are willing to pay for.

- **Tasks:**
    
    - Offer MVP packages (Launch, Scale, Dominate) to 5–10 pilot clients
        
    - Track client interest, acceptance, and objections
        
    - Test modular vs. bundled offerings
        
- **Metrics to Track:**
    
    - Conversion rate (interest → paid)
        
    - Popular packages
        
    - Feedback on pricing and service mix
        

---

## **4. Subcontractor Ecosystem Research**

**Goal:** Validate availability and quality of talent.

- **Tasks:**
    
    - Identify 10–20 local developers, designers, editors, automation specialists
        
    - Interview to capture:
        
        - Skills and experience
            
        - Rates
            
        - Reliability and availability
            
        - Interest in project-based work or revenue-sharing
            
    - Test one small project to measure quality and delivery
        
- **Metrics to Track:**
    
    - Average cost per task
        
    - Turnaround time
        
    - Quality rating (CTO review)
        
    - Willingness to work under your branding
        

---

## **5. Pricing & Monetization**

**Goal:** Determine competitive and profitable pricing.

- **Tasks:**
    
    - Compare pilot client willingness to pay with competitor pricing
        
    - Identify most profitable package mix
        
    - Test small discounts or bundles to measure impact on uptake
        
- **Metrics to Track:**
    
    - Revenue per package
        
    - Profit margin per project
        
    - Price sensitivity
        

---

## **6. Marketing Channel Research**

**Goal:** Identify the most effective way to reach clients.

- **Tasks:**
    
    - Track engagement on social media platforms: TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn
        
    - Test direct outreach (WhatsApp, calls, emails) with 20–30 leads
        
    - Identify potential partnerships (coworking spaces, associations, startup hubs)
        
- **Metrics to Track:**
    
    - Lead conversion per channel
        
    - Cost per lead (if paid ads are used)
        
    - Engagement metrics (views, clicks, shares)
        

---

## **7. Academy / Training Demand**

**Goal:** Validate interest in short courses.

- **Tasks:**
    
    - Survey students, freelancers, and junior developers on interest in courses
        
    - Pilot a free mini-workshop or webinar
        
    - Ask about preferred topics, pricing, and delivery format
        
- **Metrics to Track:**
    
    - Number of interested participants
        
    - Engagement rate in pilot sessions
        
    - Likely willingness to pay
        

---

## **8. Reporting & Analysis**

- Create a **simple dashboard** with:
    
    - Client interest vs. package
        
    - Revenue potential per service
        
    - Contractor availability & cost
        
    - Marketing effectiveness per channel
        
- Review weekly as a team to decide **which services and packages to prioritize**
