## 1. What the CEO **must** be good at (non-negotiable)

# The ceo is the conductor not the best musician


In _your_ company, the CEO is not a technical explainer. The CEO is a **decision-maker, closer, and stabilizer**.

### Core qualities required

1. **Decisiveness**
    
    - Can stop discussions and choose a direction
        
    - Comfortable saying “we’ll revisit later” and moving on
        
2. **Emotional regulation**
    
    - Handles conflict without escalation
        
    - Can de-escalate founders, clients, and contractors
        
    - Does not take disagreement personally
        
3. **Client trust creation**
    
    - Clear, confident, calm communication
        
    - Knows how to say _just enough_ to reassure clients
        
    - Knows when to pull in technical support
        
4. **Boundary setting**
    
    - Knows when to stop talking
        
    - Can cut meetings short
        
    - Can say “this is outside scope”
        
5. **Time reliability**
    
    - Available during business hours
        
    - Can respond quickly when issues arise
        
    - Not chronically overcommitted
        
6. **Authority without ego**
    
    - Comfortable leading peers
        
    - Not threatened by stronger technical minds
        
    - Does not need to “win” every argument
        
7. **Execution bias**
    
    - Prefers action over theory
        
    - Ships imperfectly but consistently
        

> The CEO’s job is not to be right.  
> The CEO’s job is to **move the company forward**.
