## **1. Color Palette**

| Purpose                           | Color          | HEX     | Notes                                                 |
| --------------------------------- | -------------- | ------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Primary / Innovation & Energy** | Electric Blue  | #1E90FF | Modern, energetic, tech-forward                       |
| **Secondary / Trust & Stability** | Deep Charcoal  | #2C2C2C | Professional, reliable, complements primary           |
| **Accent / Creativity & Growth**  | Vibrant Orange | #FF6F3C | Draws attention, highlights CTAs and key elements     |
| **Background / Clean & Neutral**  | Soft Gray      | #F5F5F5 | Minimal, modern, allows content to shine              |
| **Success / Results**             | Lime Green     | #32CD32 | Highlights achievements, metrics, or positive results |

**Why this works:**

* Blue = trust + tech
* Charcoal = professionalism + stability
* Orange = creativity, action, energy
* Soft gray = clean canvas for design
* Green = measurable success (reinforces results-oriented messaging)

---

## **2. Typography**

### **Primary Font – Headings**

* **Font:** Montserrat Bold / Semi-Bold
* **Use:** Titles, headers, CTA buttons
* **Why:** Modern, geometric, tech-savvy, highly legible

### **Secondary Font – Body / Paragraphs**

* **Font:** Lato Regular / Medium
* **Use:** Body text, paragraphs, descriptions
* **Why:** Clean, approachable, readable for both desktop and mobile

### **Accent Font – Special Highlights**

* **Font:** Poppins Medium / Semi-Bold
* **Use:** Quotes, highlights, badges
* **Why:** Friendly, contemporary, pairs well with Montserrat

---

## **3. Suggested Usage Guidelines**

* **Headers:** Montserrat Bold, Electric Blue or Charcoal
* **Subheaders / CTA Buttons:** Orange or Lime Green
* **Body Text:** Lato, Charcoal on Soft Gray background
* **Highlights / Metrics:** Lime Green or Orange to draw attention
* **Icons / Illustrations:** Use accent colors (Orange + Lime Green) for vibrancy

---

This combination gives you:

* **Modern, tech-focused aesthetic**
* **Professional credibility**
* **Energy and creativity to attract clients and academy students**
* **Scalable palette for website, social media, and print**
