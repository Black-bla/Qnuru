# 10. Risk Assessment

**Risks:**

1. Volume pressure at 60k pricing
    
2. Shared living friction
    
3. Burn without deal flow
    

**Strengths:**

1. Low payroll
    
2. Unified team
    
3. Infrastructure leverage
    
4. Realistic break-even
    

**Mitigation:** Standardized skeleton builds, template system, defined stack, SOPs, tight QA
